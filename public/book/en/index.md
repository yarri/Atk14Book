Introduction
============

ATK14 is a PHP framework for fearless boys and girls, and ATK14 Book is a book about this framework — feel free to read it even if you don't consider yourself one of them yet.
Please note that some sections are still rough drafts, others are missing entirely, and some are more detailed than strictly necessary.

Dear boys and girls, we wish you good luck and courage while reading.
